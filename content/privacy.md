# Privacy

This site is a static personal website. It does not collect, track, or share
information about visitors.

## What this site doesn't do
- No cookies are set
- No analytics or tracking scripts are loaded
- No third-party trackers are embedded
- No accounts, comments, or contact forms

## What this site does
- A single `theme` value is stored in your browser's `localStorage` if you click
  the light/dark toggle. It stays on your device and is never sent to a server.
- External links (Google Scholar, GitHub, ORCID, etc.) take you to other
  sites that have their own privacy policies.

## Server logs
The web host serving this site may keep standard access logs (IP, timestamp,
requested URL, user agent).
